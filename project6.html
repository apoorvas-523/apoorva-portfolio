Overview

NeuroFit is a motion-robust EEG wearable designed for strength athletes to deliver real-time neuroperformance feedback, recovery analytics, and sleep tracking through on-device AI processing and multi-wearable ecosystem integration.

Industry Gaps

  • EEG wearables fail in high-motion environments
  • No real-time neurofeedback during workouts
  • Cloud dependency causes latency
  • Fragmented recovery & sleep platforms
