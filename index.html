About Me

I am a Biomedical Engineering graduate student interested in developing brain-focused medical products that are safe, reliable, and human-centered. I focus on bridging design, quality, and manufacturing by translating neurological needs into manufacturable, user-friendly medical devices through risk-aware design, verification-oriented development, and usability-driven engineering. From wearable devices to software applications and 3D-printed prototypes, this portfolio demonstrates how I apply human-centered design, iterative problem-solving, and technical reasoning across diverse biomedical and engineering challenges.
